Lasagna

Description

Boil noodles in water for about 12 minutes or until soft

Separately cook ground beef and add marinara sauce til simmering

Ingridients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. boil water
  2. add noodles til soft
  3. Brown ground beef in separate pan
  4. season ground beef
  5. add marinara sauce
  6. add same seasoning
  7. cook til simmering
  8. make 4 layers alternating sauce, noodles, sauce, noodles
  9. ENJOY
